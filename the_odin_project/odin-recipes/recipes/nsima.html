NSIMA

Nsima ya Nyemba ndi Mfutso

INGREDIENTS

  • Maize Flour
  • water

PROCEDURE


  1. Pour water into cooking pot.

  2. Wait for the water to heat up until bubles appear.

  3. Pour the maize Flour slowly onto the less boiled water until it becomes less hard.

  4. Wait for it to boil for 30 minutes

  5. Add more flour to it and mix until it becomes a little hard.

  6. Cook for 5 minutes and the meal is ready.
